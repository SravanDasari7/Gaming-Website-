Marvel's Spider-Man 2

⭐ 4.3

Overview

Marvel's Spider-Man 2 is a 2023 action-adventure game developed by Insomniac Games. It follows Peter Parker
and Miles Morales as they face off against Kraven the Hunter and the Venom symbiote,
who threaten to upend their lives and the city of New York.
The Sequel to PlayStation’s critically acclaimed Marvel’s Spider-Man franchise.
